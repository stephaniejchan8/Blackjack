Blackjack

How to Play:

Each player tries to obtain a hand value as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.

Each player can choose to either:

  • HIT ME to get an additional card from the deck. Player can continue hitting unless they go bust (i.e. hand value exceeds 21).
  • STAND at their current hand value and cease receiving additional cards until the game is over.

Card Values:

  • Number cards are worth their numerical value.
  • Face cards are worth 10.
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11 (each player chooses).
